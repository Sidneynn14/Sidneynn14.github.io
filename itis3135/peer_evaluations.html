Peer Evalation 1: Pyndi, Sashank

  • There is very little content on page, also there are no pages other than the home page.
  • There is no branding element indicatiing that this is Sashank's work.
  • If the image is the center focus of the page, I think it should be a bit bigger because the words are a tad too small.
  • I think contrast in color is good. The black stands out against both the background and the light pink of the text bubbles.
  • I cannot tell what the website is about, there is no title other than "home", also the image does not make sense since their is no context.
  • While the are not many elements on the page, I would say that the elements that do exist should be aligned better to indicate some sort of design plan.


Peer Evalation 2: Teaster, Mackenzie

  • Site is well developed and theme is constant throughout.
  • Colors contrast well, the white is easily seen on the green background.
  • Font size is easily readable throughout most of the site, I would consider maybe making the paragraph on the leave a review page slightly larger for those with slight vision impairment.
  • I like that the symbols on the review page are personalized. Nice touch!
  • Content is center aligned on page, which I think suites the site well.
  • All elements are in reasonable proximity to other elements.
