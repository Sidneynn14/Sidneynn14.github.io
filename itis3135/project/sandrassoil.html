Composting From Home

Composting describes the act of recycling your food (and other biodegradable) waste into rich soil that can then be used in your plants. This can all be done from your home.

Hardiness Zones

Soil composition and temperature is different across different areas. However, for the most part, areas of similar climate have similar soil. Certain plants thrive in different soils, these soils are differentiated by their hardiness levels. And areas of similar hardiness levels are called hardiness zones. Some plants thrive outdoors in some hardniness zones but not in others, therefore it is important to know what hardiness zone you live in if you'd like to start planting outdoors.
