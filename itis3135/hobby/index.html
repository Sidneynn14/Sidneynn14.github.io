Crocheting with Sidney (About)

Hello everyone, I am Sidney Nesbitt, a sophmore computer science major at UNCC and I enjoy crocheting in my free time. I would definitly consider myself a beginner, however it is one of my most beloved hobbies.

There are many different things you can crochet. Whether that be clothes, pillows, stuffed animals, or pot holders. Its all up to your creativity and needs.

My favorite yarn to use?

My opinion may change in the begining, but as of now, I enjoy using acrylic yarn. It's easy to manipulate, doesn't pill as much, is cost effective, and is machine washable unlike some other materials.

Why Crochet?

I began crocheting in May of 2020, during the first lockdown of the Covid-19 pandemic. Crocheting is a good outlet for creativity and stress relief. I also really enjoy how crocheting allows me to use what I make. You're cold? Make a blanket. You want a new bag? You can make that too. It's a fun way for me to slow down my shopping addiction while having a hand in creating the things I use.
